About Sentinel AI

Sentinel AI is an advanced firearm detection system that leverages AlexNet deep learning architecture to identify potential threats in real time. Our mission is to protect lives by providing proactive security solutions for schools, businesses, and public spaces.

Our Technology

Using the power of convolutional neural networks, Sentinel AI analyzes live video feeds to detect firearms with exceptional speed and accuracy. Built with ethical AI principles, our system avoids facial recognition and focuses solely on object detection for privacy and safety.

Real-Time Detection

Our system identifies firearms in milliseconds, providing immediate alerts to security teams.
